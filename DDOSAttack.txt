Distributed Denial of service attack is a malicious attempt to overload a target system or server or network with heavy traffic making it slow or completely unavailble to users 
1. Types of DDoS Attacks
a. Application Layer Attacks:overwhelm web servers or application with fake requests,exhausting servers resources(eg:HTTP flood )
b. Protocol Attacks:exploit weakness in network protcols to exhaust server resources
c. Volumetric Attacks:overwhelm network bandwidth with massive amount of traffic
